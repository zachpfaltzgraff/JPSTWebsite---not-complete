Welcome To The JPST Dolphins

About the team

The Jefferson Park Swim Team was founded in 1972. We are part of the Mid-Atlantic Summer Swim League, which includes teams from the Eastern Panhandle, Maryland, Pennsylvania. Our season starts the first week after memorial day is out and lasts until the end of July. Practices are held nightly, Monday through Friday.

All Children Ages 6 to 18 Are Welcome To Join The Dolphins!

Age Groups are calculated based off of June 1st The Swim Team Fee is $150 after. There is a $10 family Discount For Every Swimmer You Register After The First Swimmer up to the fourth swimmer*

*After the fourth it is a flat rate of $120 per swimmer

Online Registration Opens Monday, March 18th and closes after the first week of june

To register a swimmer, go to the register swimmers tab and create a new swimmer
