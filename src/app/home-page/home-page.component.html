Welcome To The JPST Dolphins

About The Team

The Jefferson Park Swim Team was founded in 1972. We are part of the Mid-Atlantic Summer Swim League, which includes teams from the Eastern Panhandle, Maryland, and Pennsylvania. Our season starts the Tuesday after memorial day, and lasts until the end of July. Practices are held nightly, Monday through Thursday.

All children ages 6 to 18 are welcome to join the Dolphins!

Age groups are based on the child's birthday as of June 1st. The registration fee is $150. Family discounts apply. Online registration opens Monday, March 18th and closes after the first week of June.

To register a swimmer, go to the register swimmers tab and create a new swimmer

After you created the new swimmer, click on the "Register" button and go through the prompts
