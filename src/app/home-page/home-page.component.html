Welcome To The JPST Dolphins

About the team

The Jefferson Park Swim Team was founded in 1972. We are part of the Mid-Atlantic Summer Swim League, which includes teams from the Eastern Panhandle, Maryland, Pennsylvania. Our season starts the first week after memorial day is out and lasts until the end of July. Practices are held nightly, Monday through Friday.

All Children Ages 6 to 18 Are Welcome To Join The Dolphins!

Birthdays are calculated based off of June 1st The Swim Team Fee is $135 if you Register Before June 1st, it is $150 after. There is a $10 Discount For Every Swimmer You Register After The First Swimmer.*

*For Example, if you register before June first, your first swimmer will be $135, and your second swimmer will be $125.

Online Registration Opens April TBD and closes after the first week of june
